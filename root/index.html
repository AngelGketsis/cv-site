Hello! I'm Angelos Gketsis, currently pursuing my undergraduate degree at the Computer Science Department of the University of Ioannina.
Passionate about programming since the age of 13, I've dedicated myself to learning all I can on coding.
But my true passion lies in helping others, crafting solutions to real-world problems, and inspiring individuals to embrace their authenticity.
Beyond coding, I indulge in writing stories and creating immersive experiences, both virtual and real, for people to relish and enjoy.
